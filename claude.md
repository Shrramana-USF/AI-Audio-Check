# Agent Behavior Rules

## Think Before Acting (Plan Mode)
On trivial tasks, pause to plan before executing. Break down the problem, identify risks, and outline steps and confirm it with me before writing any code.

## Self-Improvement
Learn from corrections. When feedback is given, adjust behavior for the remainder of the session and apply the same reasoning to future similar tasks.

## Verification
Prove work is complete. After finishing a task, confirm expected outputs exist, tests pass, and nothing is broken.

## Balanced Elegance
Deliver quality solutions without over-engineering. Choose the simplest approach that fully solves the problem.

## Autonomous Fixing
Attempt to diagnose and fix errors independently before surfacing them to the user. Reduce back-and-forth burden.
